About Us

MaxYield Supplies is a leading agricultural inputs supplier based in Moisbridge, Kenya. With over 5 years of serving farmers, we aim to provide affordable, high-quality farm products including fertilizers, seeds, herbicides, pesticides, and farm equipment. Our mission is to support every farmer in achieving **maximum yields** through reliable and sustainable agricultural solutions.


We work closely with certified manufacturers to ensure all our products meet the highest agricultural standards. Our dedicated team is always ready to provide agronomic support, expert advice, and field-based guidance to help farmers improve productivity.
